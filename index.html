How Replate Works

Simple 3‑step flow for Restaurants, Events & NGOs.

1

List your surplus

Add cuisine, quantity, veg/non‑veg tags, prep time, and safe‑by window. We auto‑flag allergen info.

2

NGOs claim & schedule

Nearby NGOs get a ping. They pick a slot, assign a volunteer, and generate a pickup QR.

3

Handover & receipt

QR scan confirms handover. Both sides receive a digital receipt and basic compliance checklist.
